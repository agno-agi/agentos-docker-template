## Passing Evaluations: The Systematic Approach

### Strategy Selection for Evals
- Use strategies with **60%+ WR and <5% max drawdown**
- Risk **0.5% per trade** (half normal size)
- Target **consistency**: no single day >30% of total profits
- Spread trades across minimum required days
- **Avoid**: news trading, oversized bets, all-in days

### Top 5 Failure Patterns
1. **Daily drawdown breach** (60%+ of failures) — size too large, stop too wide
2. **Revenge trading** — emotional deviation after losses
3. **Overtrading** — too many trades, commissions eat edge
4. **Strategy-rule incompatibility** — strategy doesn't fit eval constraints
5. **Technical misunderstanding** — not understanding trailing drawdown mechanics

### Consistency Rule Navigation
- Most firms require no single day >30-50% of total profits
- Formula: Daily profit / Total profit < threshold
- **Strategy**: Make similar amounts each day. Avoid home runs.
- Example target: $300/day on $3,000 profit target = 10 days minimum

## Account Stacking & Scaling

### The Mathematics
- 1 account at $50K = $50K buying power
- 10 accounts at $50K = $500K buying power
- Trade copier replicates your trades across all accounts
- Same edge, 10x exposure

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1-2)
- Pass 1 evaluation with mastered strategy
- Trade 1 account live, build confidence
- Document performance metrics

### Phase 2: Replication (Month 3-4)
- Pass 2 more evaluations with same strategy
- Start trade copier (3 accounts)
- Verify latency and fill quality

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 5-12)
- Add accounts incrementally (1-2 per month)
- Target: 10 accounts by month 12
- Aggregate risk: never >2% across all accounts on single trade
- Monthly projection: $50K account × 10 × 2% monthly return = ~$10K/month

## Risk Management Within Prop Constraints

### Daily Loss Limit Protocol
| Firm Type | Daily Limit | Action at 75% | Action at 100% |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| Static | Fixed dollar | Reduce 50% | STOP for day |
| Trailing EOD | Follows equity | Reduce 50% | STOP for day |
| Trailing Intraday | Follows peak | Reduce 75% | STOP immediately |

### Drawdown Types Explained
- **EOD Trailing**: Drawdown resets at end of day. Most forgiving.
- **Intraday Trailing**: Follows your equity peak in real-time. Most restrictive.
- **Static**: Fixed from starting balance. Never changes.

### Emergency Protocol Near Limits
1. Reduce size 50% immediately
2. Trade only A+ setups
3. Consider stopping entirely
4. Never "one big trade to make it back"

## Building to CTA / Outside Capital

### Requirements
- 2-3 year audited track record
- NFA/CFTC registration (or 15-person exemption)
- Professional risk management documentation
- Audit services: AuditZK or similar

### The Path
1. Master personal trading (Year 1-2)
2. Build track record with audit (Year 2-3)
3. Register with NFA (if managing >$400K or >15 persons)
4. Raise capital from investors (Year 3+)
